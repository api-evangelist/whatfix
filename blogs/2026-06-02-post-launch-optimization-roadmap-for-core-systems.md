An enterprise software rollout is only the start of your application journey. It’s a milestone, but it doesn’t prove that users can consistently complete critical workflows, adopt the right features, follow approved processes, or deliver the business outcomes the system was meant to deliver. Many organizations celebrate when a new system is launched, but fail to provide the right hypercare and release-schedule roadmap to optimize workflows and enable users through frequent releases .
